Project 2: Exquisite Corpse

For our second project, we worked in pairs to create an exquisite corpse type project. Rather than base our creation off of a standard exquisite corpse which tends to focus on the human form, Kayla and I decided to work in a more landscape focused direction.

We agreed from the start that we wanted to be able to somewhat plan out our design so it would not be completely random and agreed that we wanted to create a mountain, beach and city scape. We alternated our squares in a checkerboard pattern to assure we wouldn't have two of our own squares touching.

Our corpse develops both left to right transitioning across various scenes as well as throughout the day and can also be viewed top to bottom with an increasingly more zoomed in view.

I was responsible for each of the corner squares as well as the central square and Kayla created the middle square along each of the four edges.

Each piece was created in Adobe Illustrator using a 1000 x 1000 px artboard.
